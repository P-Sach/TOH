How to Play

  • Click "Auto Solve" to watch the algorithm solve the puzzle automatically
  • The goal is to move all disks from the left tower to the right tower
  • Only one disk can be moved at a time
  • A larger disk cannot be placed on top of a smaller disk
  • Adjust the number of disks and animation speed using the sliders
